First Day at Dwarka Campus

On my first day, I visited campus via metro as it was the only convince by which I can reach to college. I read the book given by college, it was kind of manual or in layman terms a kind of code of conduct. I was excited because I was going meet all those people, with whom I interacted during online classes and did not know their faces. As I was reading book, the metro reached to Dwarka station, it stayed for 2-3 minutes there and began to move in reverse direction, it was quite shocking for me, because I might get late on that day, and when I reached Dwarka Mor station I left metro, changed the platform, and went to platform for Dwarka . I was carrying, mini drafter in my hands and bag on my back. An old man was looking at me, and I looked at him, asked him about the metro to Dwarka Sector 21. He told me that there are two routes, one is to Dwarka only, and other is to Dwarka Sector 21. The name of the train get mentioned on the board. So, it would not be the problem. After a minute or 2, metro reached to the station, and we went inside. Then, we began to talk. He asked me about my course. I told him that I am a Computer Engineering Student, and it was my first day of College. At Dwarka Sector 10, I found many students of my course there, we left for college, and stood outside for few minutes at college, because it was not opened then. Then, I went inside the college, teachers introduced themselves to us, and it was our first offline class of Engineering Drawing. Later, we went to library to get books from there. Our day, ended with IT Skills and Technology class, and we left the college at 5 PM 4:30 PM in evening.
